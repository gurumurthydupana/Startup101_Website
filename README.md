# HireHub – Verified Blue-Collar Hiring Platform

## Project Overview
HireHub is a web-based hiring platform designed to connect MSMEs and small business owners with verified blue-collar and semi-skilled workers such as electricians, plumbers, carpenters, AC technicians, and construction laborers.

The platform addresses two major problems in the hiring ecosystem:

1. Businesses face long hiring cycles, unreliable candidates, and high commissions paid to middlemen.
2. Workers often rely on agents who charge significant commissions and may provide fake job opportunities.

HireHub enables direct, verified, and commission-free hiring through a simple and mobile-friendly interface.

---

## Problem Statement
Traditional blue-collar hiring in India is highly fragmented and inefficient.

### Pain Points for Businesses
- Hiring takes 7–10+ days.
- Dependence on agents and contractors.
- High commission costs.
- Difficulty verifying worker credibility.

### Pain Points for Workers
- Agent commissions of ₹2,000–₹3,000.
- Fake job promises.
- Limited visibility to employers.
- Long job search times.

HireHub solves these challenges by creating a trusted marketplace where businesses and workers can connect directly.

---

## MVP Features (V1.0)

### For Businesses
- Post job requirements
- Browse verified worker profiles
- Filter candidates by skill and location
- Contact workers directly
- Confirm hires

### For Workers
- Register and create a profile
- Upload Aadhaar verification details
- Browse available jobs
- Apply directly

### Core Features
- Verification badges
- Toast notifications
- Mobile-friendly responsive design

The MVP was deployed using GitHub Pages.
