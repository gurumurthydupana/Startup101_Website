Key Observations
✅
What Worked Well
  • Job posting flow was intuitive
  • Verification badge built trust instantly
  • Direct hire without agent resonated strongly
  • Worker registration was completed without help
  • Mobile layout worked well for workers
🔧
Improvements Needed
  • Add regional language support (Tamil, Telugu)
  • In-app WhatsApp/call button for quick contact
  • Worker needs SMS notification on job match
  • Businesses want rating system for workers
  • Add salary range filter in worker search
MVP Hypothesis Validated ✓
Problem: MSMEs spend 7–15 days and ₹10,000–₹30,000 in agent commission to hire one verified worker.

Solution tested: Direct platform connecting verified workers and employers without middlemen.

Result: 100% of business-owner testers said they would use this platform for their next hire. 100% of worker testers said they would prefer this over agents. The core hypothesis is validated.
